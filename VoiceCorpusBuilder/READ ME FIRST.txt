============================================================
  VOICE CORPUS BUILDER  —  How to use this (Mac)
============================================================

This turns your Facebook & Instagram posts into a single text file you can
give to an AI to help it write in your voice.

Everything runs ON YOUR OWN COMPUTER. Nothing is uploaded anywhere.


------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 1 — Get your data from Facebook and Instagram
------------------------------------------------------------
Request your info in JSON format (this is important — not HTML):

FACEBOOK:
  Settings & privacy  ->  Settings  ->  Accounts Center  ->
  Your information and permissions  ->  Download your information
    - Pick your Facebook profile
    - Format: JSON          <-- IMPORTANT
    - Date range: All time
    - Include: Posts, Comments, Stories
    - Submit. Facebook emails you a download link (can take a while).

INSTAGRAM:
  Settings  ->  Accounts Center  ->
  Your information and permissions  ->  Download your information
    - Pick your Instagram profile
    - Format: JSON          <-- IMPORTANT
    - Date range: All time
    - Include: Content (posts/stories/reels) and Comments
    - Submit. Instagram emails you a download link.

You'll get one or two .zip files. You do NOT need to unzip them.


------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 2 — Put the zip files in THIS folder
------------------------------------------------------------
Drag the downloaded .zip file(s) into this same folder, right next to the
"Build Voice Corpus" icon. Facebook only, Instagram only, or both — all fine.


------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 3 — Install Python (one time, only if needed)
------------------------------------------------------------
If you don't already have Python, the app will open the download page for you.
Just download the macOS installer from python.org and click through it. You
only ever do this once.


------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 4 — Double-click "Build Voice Corpus"
------------------------------------------------------------
A window opens and asks you a few simple questions (what to include, date
range, etc.). When it finishes, your text file(s) appear in this folder.


------------------------------------------------------------
FIRST-TIME SECURITY MESSAGE (this is normal)
------------------------------------------------------------
The very first time you open it, macOS may say it "cannot be opened because
it is from an unidentified developer." This is expected for free tools.

To get past it ONCE:
  1. RIGHT-CLICK (or Control-click) the "Build Voice Corpus" icon.
  2. Choose "Open" from the menu.
  3. Click "Open" in the dialog that appears.

After that first time, you can just double-click it normally.

(If double-clicking ever does nothing: right-click "Build Voice Corpus" ->
 Open With -> Terminal.)


------------------------------------------------------------
WHAT YOU GET
------------------------------------------------------------
A text file (default name: voice_corpus.txt) with your posts in time order,
each tagged with where it came from, the date, and the type of content, so
you or the AI can filter it further.

Questions answered by the tool:
  - Which content types (posts / captions / stories / comments)
  - Date range
  - Minimum length (skip one-word and emoji-only bits)
  - Combine into one file, or one file per type

Tip: your most recent posts and captions are the best match for how you
write today.
